## 1. Instagram Algorithm 2025-2026: How Content Gets Reach

### Content Type Priority (ranked by reach potential)

| Format | Avg. Reach Multiplier | Best Use |
|--------|----------------------|----------|
| **Reels (15-60s)** | 2-3× follower count | Discovery, viral growth, top-of-funnel |
| **Carousels (2-10 slides)** | 1.5-2× | Education, saves, shares — YOUR BEST FORMAT |
| **Single Image** | 1× (baseline) | Quotes, announcements, brand moments |
| **Stories** | Followers only | Engagement, polls, link clicks, warmth |

### Algorithm Ranking Signals (2025-2026)

1. **Watch time / Dwell time** — How long someone stays on your post. Carousels win here because people swipe through multiple slides.
2. **Saves** — Instagram's #1 quality signal. Educational content gets saved. This is your superpower.
3. **Shares (DMs + Story reshares)** — The new king of distribution. Content people send to friends gets pushed to Explore.
4. **Comments** — Especially replies and conversations (not just emoji).
5. **Profile visits after viewing** — Strong signal that content drove curiosity.
6. **Follows from non-followers** — Content that converts browsers to followers gets boosted.

### Key Algorithm Changes for 2025-2026

- **"Original content" boost**: Instagram now explicitly deprioritizes reposted/aggregated content. Original graphics, original data, and original science = more reach.
- **Carousel album reach explosion**: Instagram added the ability to reshare individual carousel slides to Stories, and the algorithm now re-serves carousels to people who didn't swipe through all slides (second impression cycle). This makes carousels incredibly powerful for educational content.
- **Small account boost**: Accounts under 10K followers get proportionally more Explore/Reels placement than large accounts — the window is NOW.
- **SEO in captions**: Instagram search is now keyword-based. Putting searchable terms in your captions (not just hashtags) matters. Write captions like mini-articles.
- **Threads cross-posting**: Cross-posting to Threads gives a small boost on Instagram. Worth doing.
- **Collaborative posts**: Collab posts (2 accounts, 1 post) get 2× the normal reach because they appear in both accounts' feeds.
- **DM engagement signals**: Accounts that generate DM conversations rank higher. CTAs that drive DMs ("DM me 'QUIZ' for your strain match") are extremely effective.

### What This Means for MyStrainAI

Carousels are your #1 format. They're educational, they drive saves, they get re-served, and they don't require video production. Reels are important for discovery but you can make simple motion-graphic Reels from carousel-style slides using Instagram's built-in tools or Canva. You don't need to be on camera.

## 2. Cannabis Industry on Instagram: Policies, Risks & What Works

### Instagram's Cannabis Policy (Current as of 2025-2026)

**What's ALLOWED:**
- Education about cannabis (strains, terpenes, effects, science)
- Cannabis technology and software (apps, tools)
- Lifestyle content related to cannabis culture
- Advocacy and policy discussions
- Linking to informational websites (NOT direct sales)
- Discussing cannabinoids, terpenes, and pharmacology in educational context
- User testimonials about apps/tools (not about getting high)

**What's PROHIBITED (and will get you restricted/banned):**
- Direct sale of cannabis or facilitating transactions
- Price lists, menu boards, "shop now" language for cannabis
- Images of consumption (smoking, vaping, dabbing)
- Images of cannabis flower, concentrates, edibles in a "for sale" context
- Claims of medical benefits ("cures anxiety", "treats pain")
- Content targeting minors
- Promoting illegal activity in jurisdictions where cannabis is illegal

**What's in the GRAY ZONE (proceed carefully):**
- Images of cannabis plants (generally fine if educational, not sales)
- Strain review content (fine as education, risky if it reads like sales)
- "Where to buy" content (avoid)
- Hashtags that are periodically banned (#weed, #marijuana often get restricted)

### MyStrainAI's Advantage

You are a **cannabis tech/education platform**, not a dispensary or brand. This gives you significantly more latitude because:
- You're promoting a software tool, not a drug
- Your content is science-based and educational
- You don't sell cannabis
- Your quiz is about personalization, not getting high

**Position every post as education/technology, not cannabis promotion.**

### What Successful Cannabis Brands Do on Instagram

**Brands crushing it (study these accounts):**
- **@leafly** — Master of strain education carousels, terpene graphics, and effect-based content
- **@weedmaps** — Lifestyle + education hybrid, strong Reels game
- **@dosist** — Clean, clinical aesthetic (closest to your vibe), wellness positioning
- **@cannaclusive** — Community-first approach, strong engagement
- **@haborside** — Education-forward dispensary content
- **@thecannabisindustry** — B2B cannabis, clean infographic style

**Common traits of successful cannabis accounts:**
1. Education-first framing (not "buy this," but "learn this")
2. Clean, branded templates (consistent visual identity)
3. Heavy use of carousels for strain/terpene education
4. Community engagement (responding to every comment)
5. Careful language (say "wellness" not "medical," "experience" not "high")
6. Never show prices or sales CTAs for actual cannabis

### Shadowban Prevention

**Shadowbanning indicators:**
- Sudden drop in reach to non-followers
- Posts not appearing in hashtag feeds
- Reduced engagement from non-followers

**Prevention tactics:**
1. Never use currently banned hashtags (see Section 4)
2. Don't use more than 20 hashtags (Instagram's recommendation is 3-5 highly relevant ones, but 15-20 still works for growth accounts)
3. Mix hashtag sets between posts (don't copy-paste the same set)
4. Avoid "engagement bait" language ("like if you agree," "tag a friend")
5. Don't use link shorteners (Instagram flags them)
6. Post from the app, not third-party schedulers initially (build trust with the algorithm)
7. If shadowbanned: stop posting for 48 hours, remove flagged hashtags, switch to new sets

## 9. Carousel & Static Image Strategy (No Video Production Needed)

### Why Carousels Are Your Best Format

- **Highest save rate** of any Instagram format (educational content gets bookmarked)
- **Highest share rate** after Reels (people DM carousels to friends)
- **Double-impression cycle**: Instagram re-shows carousels to people who didn't swipe all slides
- **No production required**: Just good design templates + your actual data
- **Long dwell time**: 10-slide carousel = 30-60 seconds of attention
- **SEO value**: More text in alt-text fields → better discoverability

### Carousel Architecture: The Perfect 10-Slide Formula

**Slide 1 — THE HOOK (most important slide)**
- Bold headline that creates curiosity or challenges an assumption
- Dark background with high-contrast text
- No logos, no clutter — just the hook
- Examples:
  - "Indica vs Sativa is meaningless. Here's what actually matters."
  - "The 3 molecules that predict whether a strain will relax you"
  - "We analyzed 24,853 strains. Here's what we found."
  - "Your budtender is guessing. Here's the science."

**Slide 2 — THE CONTEXT**
- Set up the problem or question
- "Most people choose strains based on indica/sativa labels. But here's the thing..."

**Slides 3-8 — THE CONTENT**
- One idea per slide
- Large text (readable on mobile without zooming)
- Data visualizations, charts, molecular diagrams
- Each slide should make sense on its own (for Story reshares)

**Slide 9 — THE PAYOFF**
- Summary or "so what does this mean for you?"
- Connect the education back to the user's experience

**Slide 10 — THE CTA**
- "Take the free quiz → link in bio"
- Or "Save this for your next dispensary visit 📌"
- Or "Share this with someone who needs to hear this"
- Include @mystrainai handle and branded hashtag

### Design Specifications

**Dimensions:** 1080 × 1350px (4:5 portrait — takes up maximum feed space)

**Font system:**

- Data people want to remember
- Step-by-step guides
- Cheat sheets and quick-reference cards

**Triggers for SHARES (DMs):**
- "Send this to someone who always picks the wrong strain"
- Controversial takes people want friends' opinions on
- Quiz results and personality-type content
- Surprising data that makes people say "I had no idea"
- Strain recommendations for specific occasions ("for the concert," "for the creative session")

### "Reel-Style" Content Without Video

You can create Reels without filming anything:

1. **Slideshow Reels**: Use Instagram's built-in tool to turn 5-7 images into a Reel with transitions and trending audio — takes 2 minutes
2. **Text-on-screen Reels**: Use Canva to create animated text slides exported as MP4 — looks like a produced Reel
3. **Screen recording Reels**: Screen-record yourself using the MyStrainAI quiz/app with voiceover or text overlay
4. **Data animation Reels**: Animate a chart building (Canva has this) — "Watch the terpene profile build for Blue Dream"
5. **Ken Burns on infographics**: Take a single infographic, add slow pan/zoom, add music = Reel

## 13. Language Guide: Safe Cannabis Vocabulary

### Use These Terms:
- "Wellness" (not "medical")
- "Experience" (not "high")
- "Effects" (not "getting stoned/baked")
- "Consume" (not "smoke/hit/rip")
- "Personalized matching" (not "drug recommendation")
- "Community-reported data" (not "user reviews of drugs")
- "Molecular profiles" (not "dank terps")
- "Cannabis science" / "Plant science"
- "Receptor pharmacology" / "Terpene pharmacology"
- "Strain finder" / "Strain matcher"
- "Science-based" / "Evidence-based" / "Data-driven"

### Never Use:
- Medical claims ("cures," "treats," "heals")
- Drug slang in official posts
- "Buy" / "Purchase" / "Shop" (for cannabis itself)
- "Dose" / "Dosing" (medical framing)
- Price references for cannabis products
- "Recreational" (unnecessary and can trigger review)
